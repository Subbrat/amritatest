The Science of Life !







"Wonders Through
Microscope"

​From the molecular level of a cell, to the anatomy and physiology of organisms, and the evolution of populations - Biology is Dynamic. Seeking to populate this sterile universe with living creatures, God chose the elegant mechanism of evolution to create microbes, plants, and animals of all sorts. My jaws just drop in fascination, pondering the incredible complexity of the Human Body. Research in Neuroscience and psychology of consciousness is inspiring. The advancement in Medical Biology and Public Health, has contributed to vaccination, neutralizing antibodies, early detection and treatment of deadly diseases. If there's ever been a moment in my life where it's clear how important Immunology is as that torch, it's been during this pandemic.

My goal and challenge - is to search for truth and knowledge through articles, research and explore discoveries, and create a social awareness through events conducted for general public.
